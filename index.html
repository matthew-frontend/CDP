WHAT IS

Design students live in a constant state of rush-racing against deadlines, juggling creativity and execution, and navigating the chaotic traffic of ideas, revisions, and sleepless nights.

Design Rush Hour" captures this high-energy, high-stakes journey, drawing parallels between the frenzied process of submitting work and the adrenaline rush of a busy city in motion.
